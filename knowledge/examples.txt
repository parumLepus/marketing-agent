EXAMPLE: High-performing LinkedIn Post

Content:
“I grew our email list from 0 to 10,000 in 90 days. No ads. No influencers. Just this system: [steps] → Repost if this helped.”

Why it works:

Specific numbers (credibility)
Clear transformation (0 → 10k)
Removes common objections (no ads, no influencers)
Curiosity loop (system hidden)
Engagement CTA (repost)

Pattern:
Big result → constraints removed → system tease → CTA
