TEMPLATE: LinkedIn Hook Formula

Use this to grab attention in the first line.
Pattern: Controversial statement + Proof + Promise
Example: “Most B2B founders waste 80% of their ad budget. Here’s what the top 1% do differently.”

TEMPLATE: Cold Email Subject Line

Keep under 8 words. Personal + specific.
Pattern: Pain + Specific insight
Example: “3 reasons your leads aren’t converting”

TEMPLATE: Cold Email Opening

Use for outbound sales emails.
Pattern: Personalisation + Observation + Relevance
Example: “Noticed your team is scaling hiring — usually that’s when lead quality starts to drop. Thought this might help.”

TEMPLATE: Sales Email Structure

Use for conversion-focused emails.
Pattern: Hook → Problem → Solution → Proof → CTA
Example:

Hook: “You’re losing leads daily”
Problem: explain friction
Solution: introduce fix
Proof: results or case study
CTA: book call / reply
TEMPLATE: Landing Page Structure

Use for high-conversion landing pages.
Pattern: Hero → Problem → Solution → Benefits → Social Proof → CTA
Example:
Clear headline → pain amplification → solution intro → benefits list → testimonials → CTA button

TEMPLATE: Instagram / LinkedIn Caption (B2B)

Start strong, tell story, end with action.
Pattern: Hook → Problem → Story → Lesson → CTA
Example: “We almost lost 90% of leads in 30 days. Here’s what we fixed → [story] → Save this if you’re scaling.”

TEMPLATE: Ad Copy (Paid Social)

Use for Meta / LinkedIn ads.
Pattern: Hook → Pain → Outcome → CTA
Example: “Still struggling with low-quality leads? Here’s how B2B brands fix it in 14 days → Book a demo.”

TEMPLATE: Value Proposition Formula

Use for positioning clarity.
Pattern: We help [audience] achieve [result] without [pain]
Example: “We help real estate brands generate high-quality leads without wasting budget on low-intent traffic.”

TEMPLATE: Case Study Story

Use for credibility building.
Pattern: Problem → Intervention → Result → Insight
Example: “Client had low conversion rates → we redesigned funnel → leads increased 62% → key insight: messaging mismatch was the issue.”

TEMPLATE: CTA Formula

Use for all conversion points.
Pattern: Action verb + benefit + urgency
Example: “Get your free strategy breakdown in 24 hours”
